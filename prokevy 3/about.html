About Me

This site is a gallery of my work. Feel free to look at all of my pieces. If you have any questions, feel free to contact me.

In 2020 I went Fairleigh Dickinson Univeristy Florham Park Campus, but after two semesters Fall, I took a break from school to focus on my mental health. I only took Fall 2020 and Fall 2021.
